關於我們｜About Us

音韻合唱團是一個以成人團員為主的合唱團， 成立宗旨在於透過合唱音樂，建立彼此支持、共同成長的音樂社群。

我們重視音樂品質，也同樣珍惜排練過程中的交流與陪伴， 讓歌聲成為生活中溫柔而穩定的力量。

Harmonic Choir is an adult choir dedicated to building a warm and supportive community through choral music.

We believe in both musical excellence and human connection, allowing harmony to become a meaningful part of everyday life.

入團辦法｜Join Us

歡迎喜愛合唱、願意投入固定排練的成人朋友加入。 是否具備合唱經驗皆可，重點是對音樂的熱情與學習的心。

報名流程： 填寫申請表 → 試唱 → 排練試班 → 正式加入

聯絡我們

We welcome adult singers who enjoy choral music and value consistent rehearsal and collaboration. Prior experience is helpful but not required.

Application Process: Fill application → Audition → Trial rehearsals → Official membership
